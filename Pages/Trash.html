Operations & Sales Automation Project

Project Manager Portfolio

Project Overview

Led the design and implementation of an automation solution to streamline operations and sales processes. The project focused on reducing manual tasks, improving data accuracy, and accelerating sales cycles for enhanced business efficiency.

Key Responsibilities

  • Coordinated cross-functional teams including sales, operations, and IT.
  • Analyzed existing workflows to identify automation opportunities.
  • Developed project timelines, milestones, and deliverables.
  • Oversaw integration of CRM and ERP systems for seamless data flow.
  • Managed stakeholder communications and project documentation.

Results & Impact

  • Reduced manual data entry by 70% through automated workflows.
  • Shortened sales cycle time by 30%.
  • Improved reporting accuracy and real-time visibility for management.
  • Increased team productivity and customer satisfaction.
